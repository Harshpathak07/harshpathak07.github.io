INDUSTRIAL​

Hotels have become places of business, pleasure, activity, quality family time, places to hold family & business events etc. etc. They fulfill an important basic need of the society and hence have to be carefully architected and designed. Our team consults extensively with the stakeholders to understand their values and philosophy and the kind of environment they would like to create in the hotel. Every small detail is noted and we try and blend in as many features as possible in the allocated budget to enhance the brand value and position the hotel suitably. We take turn-key projects where we not only design and develop the hotel buildings but also do the room interiors, conference room, the lobby or reception, gym & spa, swimming pool etc. and the entire landscaping included. Our clients can be rest assured about the quality of work and our quest to attain perfection.

Shading

We must make allowances for shaded areas where the public (customers, visitors or clients) can rest or find comfort. Summer seasons and even rainy ones make this feature an absolute must-have in any commercial architecture venture.

Ventilation

Proper spatial organization also means ideal ventilation to promote good air flow inside your building and thereby get rid of stuffiness, mustiness and other uncomfortable settings.

Greenery

We can’t be remiss about the influence of plant life, including lawns and flowers. Sometimes taking a garden atop a sloping roof can be both appealing as well as productive to the ‘green’ side of your project.

Natural Lighting

It’s one thing to collaborate with engineers and electricians to set up outstanding lighting features in your commercial building but it’s another thing to take excellent advantage of natural light. The sun is the first thing that leaps to mind whereby we design your public edifice to usher in beautiful sunlight with none of the heat and glare that accompanies it. This in itself is one of the most valuable features we work with on our projects

Structural integrity

We focus on both the ceiling and all the architectural elements that go with it as well as the floor/basement in like manner. The scale of your commercial building will determine how we drawn up these spaces based on your requirements and other technical factors. We even work with underground/open-air parking lots.

Paint & Furnishings

We come trained to offer advice on the type of paint that will best suit your commercial building. In addition, if you so need it, we offer consultation on how effectively you can furnish your interiors so it makes your customers/clients/visitors feel great just being inside.

Planning and Administration

We get to work on site, where assessments and studies are carried out as needed. Afterwards, our team takes into consideration the lay of the land, current and future climactic changes and environment-friendly technology and functioning. Using these and other landscape factors, we’re confident that we can help you raise a commercial or public edifice that will truly serve its purpose for many years to come Our designs are drawn based on these and other ideals. This is one of the most important sectors where our architects can make a statement, leave a mark or both.
