Profile


Practice

Spatial Designs Consultants Pvt. Ltd. is a progressive architectural practice setup in 1983 with a wide spectrum of projects and experience.

Ranging from 5 star hotels, hospitals, IT, Corporate Offices, Multiplexes to School, Factories, Mass Residential and private bungalows, the firm has designed various landmark projects across the country.

Spatial Designs Consultants Pvt. Ltd. is recognized and referred by its Clients for Quality and Dependability.
Philosophy

Spatiality - Optimized Space Planning
Simplicity - Avoiding Cosmetic Elements
Sustainability - Sensitive & Feasible
are the 3 – S that summarize the firm’s philosophy and practice.

Big or Small, every project is treated as a new challenge and opportunity to create and cater to the needs of the society, so is dealt with care, diligence and optimism.

The firm focuses on 3 – Dimensions viz. Design, Detail and Deliver with Dedication adding to the 4th Dimension.
Process

The emphasis is laid on evolving a holistic design solution with a balanced form and composition that is synthesized from a streamlined approach engaged in spatial and functional planning.

The design process involves Site Visit, Initial Analysis, Schematic Design, Design Development, Coordination, Detailing, Documentation and Interim Site Visits till completion.

The firm is technically well equipped and its architects are affluent with latest trends, technologies and regulations.
