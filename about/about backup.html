Practice

Established in 1983, we are a progressive architectural firm with a wide spectrum of projects to our credit.

Hospitality and Health-care Design being the core areas of specialization, the firm also undertakes various projects Commercial, IT, Residential, Industrial and Institutional across the country.

Simplicity, Sustainability and Streamlined approach constitute the firm’s design philosophy and practice. The focus is on synthesizing efficient design models with optimum space planning, neat detailing and poised form evolved out of the functional requirements.
